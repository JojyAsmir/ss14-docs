[head=2]Inaprovaline - Crit[/head]
[italic]Base ingredient in other chems. Make this first! Can also be used to aid patients suffering asphyxiation as a result of critical injury.
[bold]Do not mix with Dylovene![/bold][/italic]
[bullet/] 1 oxygen
[bullet/] 1 carbon
[bullet/] 1 sugar

[head=2]Bicardine - Brute - 15u OD[/head]
[italic]Basic medicine for physical trauma, such as bruises, cuts, and stab wounds. A prerequisite for more powerful, specialized chems.
[bold]Do not mix with Bruiz/Lace/Punc![/bold][/italic]
[bullet/] 1 inaprovaline
[bullet/] 1 carbon

[head=2]Dylovene  - Poison - 20u OD[/head]
[italic]Basic but effective anti-poison medication. A prerequisite for more advanced chems.
[bullet/] 1 nitrogen
[bullet/] 1 potassium
[bullet/] 1 sillicon

[head=2]Kelotane[/head]
[italic]Base ingredient in chems that treat burns.
[bold]Not medicine! Do not use on patients![/bold][/italic]
[bullet/] 1 sillicon
[bullet/] 1 carbon

[head=2]Dermaline - Burns - 10u OD[/head]
[italic]Basic medicine that will heal burns related to both extreme heat and extreme cold. Will not treat caustic chemical burns, however.[/italic]
[bullet/] 1 kelotane
[bullet/] 1 oxygen
[bullet/] 1 phosphorous
